Support

A gift to Engineers Without Borders at the University of Kentucky advances practical, community‑centered engineering solutions in regions facing urgent challenges. your support enables student teams to implement resilient infrastructure, expand access to clean water and energy, and build local capacity through collaborative partnerships. funding helps projects move through essential stages from design and materials to testing and training, ensuring that innovative concepts become sustainable and long‑lasting systems.
